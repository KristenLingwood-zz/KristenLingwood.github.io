About Me

Welcome to my little corner of the world wide web!

I’m Kristen and I love reading, puzzles, and games. I’m also super passionate about music and I love to travel. Once when I was geeking out over how fun an escape room was, someone told me “you know, there are people who solve problems for a living. They’re called engineers”. They suggested that I check out codecademy, and as soon as I built my first rock paper scissors game complete with an easter egg “bug”, I was hooked. Realizing I could leverage problem solving and logic with my love of learning and creating drove me to pursue coding as a career.

I'm a deeply curious person. Every time I ask a question, the answer leads me to more and better questions. This directly informs my style of problem solving-- find out what I know, what I don't know, and what I need to do to bridge the gap. I'm motivated by things that are challenging and I can't wait to learn more, about everything, every day.

Please feel free to reach out. I'd love to talk code, or books, or travel, or board games, or learn something new from you!
